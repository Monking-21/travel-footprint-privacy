Privacy Policy

App: GoGoGo (Travel Footprint, iOS)
Developer: Monking
Last updated: June 10, 2026

In short: all of this app's data — location, routes, check-ins, photos, and videos — stays on your device. The developer does not collect, upload, or share any of your data with anyone. The app contains no network requests, no accounts, no ads, and no tracking.

How we use location

Location is used to identify the place you are checking in at, and to build your travel route after you actively tap "Start Route". The app never tracks your location silently when no route is being recorded.

Background location

Only when you grant "Always" location permission and actively start a route recording will the app keep recording while the screen is locked or you switch apps. Recording stops immediately when you tap pause or end.

Stored locally, never uploaded

Your location data, routes, check-ins, and media files are stored only on this device. Nothing is uploaded to any server, and nothing is used for advertising or tracking. The app has no networking capability at all.

Camera, microphone, and photo library

The camera, microphone, and photo library are accessed only when you actively take a photo, record a video, or pick media from your library. Copies of media you select are stored only on this device.

Data sharing

We do not collect your data, so there is nothing to share with third parties. The app includes no third-party SDKs, analytics, or advertising services.

Your controls

In the app's "Settings & Privacy" you can export a copy of your data or permanently erase all local data at any time. You can also change location, camera, microphone, and photo permissions in the system Settings whenever you like. Deleting the app deletes all of its local data.

Changes to this policy

If a future version introduces new data uses (for example, optional cloud sync), we will update this policy and clearly notify you inside the app before shipping such features.

隐私政策

适用应用：GoGoGo（旅行足迹 / Travel Footprint，iOS）
开发者：Monking
最后更新：2026年6月10日

一句话总结：本 App 的所有数据（位置、轨迹、打卡、照片和视频）仅保存在你的设备本机。开发者不收集、不上传、不与任何第三方共享你的任何数据。本 App 不含任何网络请求、账号系统、广告或追踪。

我们如何使用位置

位置用于识别当前打卡地点，以及在你主动点击"开始路线"后生成旅行轨迹。本 App 不会在你未开始路线时静默持续定位。

后台定位

只有当你选择"始终允许"定位权限并主动开始路线记录后，App 才会在锁屏或切换应用时继续记录轨迹。点击暂停或结束后，位置采集会立即停止。

本地保存，不上传

你的位置、轨迹、打卡记录和媒体文件仅保存在此设备，不会自动上传任何服务器，也不会用于广告或追踪。本 App 没有任何联网功能。

相机、麦克风与相册

只有在你主动拍摄照片、录制视频或从相册选择媒体时，App 才会访问相机、麦克风与相册。你选择的媒体副本仅保存在此设备。

信息共享

我们不收集你的数据，因此也不存在与第三方共享的情形。App 内不集成任何第三方 SDK、统计或广告服务。

你的控制权

你可以在 App 的"设置与隐私"中随时导出数据副本，或彻底清除全部本机数据；也可以随时在系统设置中修改位置、相机、麦克风和相册权限。删除 App 即会删除其保存在本机的全部数据。

政策变更

如果未来版本引入新的数据用途（例如可选的云同步），我们会先更新本政策并在 App 内显著提示，再上线相关功能。
